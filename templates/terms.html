General Rules

1. Respect All Players

Treat all players with respect. Harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior will not be tolerated.

2. No Inappropriate Content

Keep all content appropriate for all ages. This includes usernames, skins, builds, and chat messages.

3. English Only in Global Chat

Please use English in global chat to ensure all players can understand and participate.

4. No Advertising

Do not advertise other servers, Discord servers, or any external services without permission.

Chat Rules

1. No Spam or Flooding

Avoid repeating messages, using excessive caps, or flooding chat with unnecessary messages.

2. No Swearing or Profanity

Keep chat clean and family-friendly. Excessive swearing or bypassing chat filters is prohibited.

3. No Controversial Topics

Avoid discussing politics, religion, or other controversial topics that may cause arguments.

Gameplay Rules

1. No Cheating or Hacking

The use of hacked clients, unfair modifications, or exploits is strictly prohibited.

2. No Griefing

Do not destroy, steal, or modify other players' builds without permission.

3. No AFK Farming

Using AFK methods to gain unfair advantages is not allowed.

4. Report Bugs

If you find a bug or exploit, report it to staff immediately. Do not abuse it.
